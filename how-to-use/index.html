05
Running the Monday Meeting
45 minutes. Four phases. No exceptions.
1
Before the call starts (2 min)
Open the dashboard. Click ‚ö° MONDAY MEETING in the header. Confirm all three reports are filed ‚Äî check the right sidebar. If someone hasn't filed, they speak last.
2
Phase 1 ‚Äî Metrics Review (10 min)
Each founder reads their 3 key numbers from their report. No context, no excuses, no discussion ‚Äî just the numbers. If a metric is red it gets noted. Debate happens in Phase 3.
3
Phase 2 ‚Äî Commitments Check (10 min)
Pull up last week's commitments in the right sidebar. Go through each one. Binary: done or not done. No partial credit. No "almost." Check them off or leave them red.
4
Phase 3 ‚Äî Blockers (15 min)
Go through the open blockers in the sidebar. Every blocker must leave this phase with: a problem statement, an owner, and a deadline. If you can't assign it, it's not a blocker ‚Äî it's your task.
5
Phase 4 ‚Äî Next Week Commitments (10 min)
Each founder states exactly 3 deliverables for the week. Not tasks ‚Äî deliverables. Things that will exist and can be verified. Type them into the Submit tab before the call ends.
What Not To Do In The Meeting No brainstorming. No strategy debates. No "what if we tried..." conversations. If someone raises a strategic question, the answer is: "File it as a task for after the meeting." The meeting is for execution review only.

07
Glossary
Terms used in the dashboard and what they mean.
Design Partner
A real company paying to use REVOS before the software is built. They validate the model manually. 5 signed = engineers get hired. This is the company's #1 priority.
Output
Something that now exists in the world and can be verified. A delivered call, a published page, a signed document, a completed analysis. Not effort. Not time spent.
Blocker
A specific problem that prevents progress, that requires another founder to solve. Format: Problem ‚Üí Owner ‚Üí Deadline. Not a complaint ‚Äî a request with accountability.
Streak
The number of consecutive weeks a founder has submitted their report on time. Breaking the streak is a yellow flag. A streak of zero for two weeks triggers a plan review.
Stale Deal
An active prospect in the pipeline that hasn't been touched in 5+ days. Shows red in the pipeline board and triggers an alert. Filipe must act on the same day it goes red.
Launch Blocker
A roadmap deliverable marked with a red BLOCKER badge. These items gate the launch ‚Äî the company cannot move to the next phase without completing them.
Commitment
A deliverable you state at the end of Monday's meeting. Not a task or intention ‚Äî a specific output you will produce by Friday. Maximum 3 per founder per week.
REVOS Phase
The pre-launch roadmap has 3 phases: Foundation (Weeks 1‚Äì3), Market Entry (Weeks 4‚Äì8), and Pre-Software Validation (Weeks 9‚Äì12). Each phase has a different primary objective.
